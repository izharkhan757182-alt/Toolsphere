Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what happens to your data when you use ToolsPhere.

Tool data

Every tool on this site — the QR code generator, word counter, password generator, and all others — runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Text you type, files you generate, and passwords you create are never uploaded to our servers or any third party. Closing or refreshing the page clears everything.

Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics to understand which tools are used most, such as page views and general location (country-level). This data is aggregated and not tied to your individual tool inputs.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use tracking cookies to build advertising profiles.

Third-party resources

Some tools load small code libraries from third-party content delivery networks (CDNs) to function, such as the QR code rendering library. These requests do not include your tool input data.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via our contact page.
