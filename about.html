About us

Simple tools, done properly.

ToolsPhere started with a simple frustration: most "free tool" websites are cluttered with pop-ups, fake download buttons, and ads disguised as buttons. We wanted a place where a tool just works — fast, clean, and without tricks.

Every tool on this site runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you type, upload, or generate is sent to a server or stored anywhere — your QR codes, passwords, and text stay on your device.

We're actively building out more tools across text, calculators, converters, and developer utilities. If there's a tool you wish existed here, let us know.
